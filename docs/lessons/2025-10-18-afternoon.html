Lead

Factors the general conic’s quadratic part as a(x - λ₁y + A)(x - λ₂y + B) and reduces the shift terms to a 2×2 linear system for A, B.

Key theorems and identities (from the session)

  • ax² + bxy + cy² = a(x - λ₁y)(x - λ₂y) with λᵢ from the quadratic formula
  • A + B = d, -(Bλ₁ + Aλ₂) = e/f reduce to 2×2 linear system
