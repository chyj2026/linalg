Lead

Extends the tan(2θ) = 2b/(a-c) symmetric-axis formula from hyperbolas to ellipses by reinterpreting bounding-box diagonal slopes as complex k₁,k₂ obtained by substituting yi for y.

Key theorems and identities (from the session)

  • tan(2θ) = 2b/(a-c) holds for both elliptical and hyperbolic conics
  • Box-diagonal interpretation of symmetric axes via yi substitution

Homework given

Important
